Cody Cook

Biography

Welcome! I am a 4th-year PhD candidate in Economics at Stanford GSB. My main fields are Public/Urban and Industrial Organization; some of my research interests include neighborhood amenities, affordable housing polices, and congestion pricing.

I grew up in New Hampshire and did my undergraduate at the University of Chicago. Before coming to Stanford, I worked on a research team at Uber.

If you have any questions or thoughts about my research, my email is codycook@stanford.edu.
